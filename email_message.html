Rankings & Ratings at HDPS

To help patients decide where to receive care, our country generates hospital-clinic rankings by evaluating data on nearly 5,000 hospitals and clinics in 16 adult medical specialties and 9 adult medical procedures or conditions. To be nationally ranked in a specialty, a clinic must excel in caring for the sickest, most medically complex patients. The ratings in procedures and conditions, by contrast, focus on typical Medicare patients. HDPS is ranked nationally in 12 adult specialties . It also achieved the highest rating possible in 9 procedures or conditions.
